Fish Curry

Fish Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 500 grams fish (such as pomfret, kingfish, or tilapia, cleaned and cut)
  2. 2 tablespoons oil
  3. 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  4. 1 onion (finely chopped)
  5. 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  6. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  7. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  8. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  9. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  10. 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  11. 1 cup coconut milk
  12. 1 cup water
  13. Salt to taste
  14. Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  15. Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions:

  • Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  • Stir in tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Mix well.
  • Add coconut milk and water, then bring the curry to a gentle boil.
  • Gently place the fish pieces into the curry. Cover and simmer for 10-15 minutes or until the fish is fully cooked.
  • Add salt to taste and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice and lemon wedges.
