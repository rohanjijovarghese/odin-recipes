Pizza : chicken sausage pizza

Recipe

Ingredients

:
  1. 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  2. 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  3. 1 cup cooked chicken sausage (sliced)
  4. 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  5. 1/2 red onion (thinly sliced)
  6. 1/2 bell pepper (sliced)
  7. Olive oil (for brushing)
  8. Dried oregano (optional)
  9. Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Instructions

:
  • Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  • Prepare the Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  • Add Sauce: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  • Add Toppings:
    • Layer the cooked chicken sausage slices, red onion, and bell pepper on top of the sauce.
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the toppings.
  • Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Finish: Brush the crust with a little olive oil for a golden shine. Optionally, sprinkle with dried oregano and fresh basil leaves for added flavor.
  • Serve: Slice and enjoy your homemade chicken sausage pizza!
